Protection Relays

DaiTinEnergy protection relays provide a wide range of solutions for protecting power systems, ensuring the safety, reliability, and efficiency needed for safe operation. Resulting from advanced engineering, they ensure intervention in case of faults or anomalies in the electrical network, thus preventing untimely service interruptions and the consequent material and economic damages, ensuring the safety of people and equipment.

FEEDER PROTECTION RELAYS

Protection relays for distribution plants and transmission lines.
MOTOR PROTECTION RELAYS

Protection and control relays for asynchronous motors of all power levels, for any driving logic.
TRANSFORMER PROTECTION

Protection relays for power and distribution transformers with two or three windings.
GENERATOR PROTECTION RELAYS

Protection and control relays for generators of any power level in hydroelectric, steam, and thermal plants.
GRID INTERCONNECTION PROTECTION

Protection relays for connecting active and passive users to the distribution network, compliant with CEI 0-16 italian technical standards.
CAPACITOR BANK PROTECTION RELAYS

The specific solution for single or double capacitor banks with Wye grounding (or isolated from ground) and harmonic filters.
SECONDARY MEDIUM VOLTAGE PROTECTION RELAYS

Self-powered protection relays and advanced fault detectors designed for the protection and automation of medium voltage networks with any type of grounding.

Integrated Solutions for the Automation and Monitoring of Electrical Networks: RTU, BCU, Gateway, SCADA, CCI, UPDM

The products and technologies for automation, supervision, and control of electrical networks are the optimal solution for comprehensively and safely managing electrical networks. From small distribution substations to complex transmission systems, this technology guarantees maximum scalability and, regardless of the size or complexity of the electrical infrastructure, provides an efficient response to all operational needs.

RTU

A new integrated and modular platform designed for the automation and remote control of High Voltage (HV) and Medium Voltage (MV) distribution substations.
BAY CONTROL UNIT

Advanced and compact control units with high-density I/O, multi-protocol support, AC/DC analog measurements, color HMI, and digital fault recording.
GATEWAY

The smart, configurable platform for substation automation, data and event summarization, protocol conversion.
SCADA

SCADA for substation automation, energy distribution systems, and automated control of the generation process.

DISCOVER XMORE

More advanced, High-End IEDs for protecting, monitoring, and controlling Electric Power Systems

The XMORE platform is the latest DaiTinEnergy product line to protect power generation, transmission and distribution and covers all energy market solutions: Utility, Renewable Generation, Oil&Gas, Transportation and Large energy-consuming industry. Highly flexible and scalable in terms of performance, XMORE offers tailor-made solutions thanks to the modularity of the hardware and software components.
