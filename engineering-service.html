ADVANCED ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS FOR ELECTRICAL NETWORK PROTECTION AND AUTOMATION

With solid experience and deep knowledge of the electrical energy sector, DaiTinEnergy offers automation engineering services for a wide range of clients, including OEMs, EPCs, and transmission and distribution operators. Our engineering team is dedicated to the design and implementation of new systems, retrofits, and adaptations to existing plants, tackling and solving any integration challenges.

Thanks to the extensive experience of our technicians, we ensure the application of the highest quality standards at every project stage. From detailed customer requirements analysis, through preliminary and detailed design, to development, testing, and commissioning, our goal is to optimize the protection and control of electrical networks.

Our services include:

  • Component specifications
  • Mechanical drawings
  • Electrical diagrams
  • Engineering and production of control and protection systems
  • Fully documented logic programs
  • Third-party device integration and compatibility testing
  • On-site installation and commissioning

Through our dedicated and personalized approach, we are committed to providing solutions that not only meet but anticipate market needs, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and innovation in energy distribution and transmission networks.

Solutions with IEC61850 Standard

In the field of electrical systems engineering, DaiTinEnergy stands out for its deep expertise and cutting-edge services, effectively meeting the modernization and digitalization needs of the energy sector. The shift towards digitalization and the need to address complex challenges in the energy sector have led to the definition of the IEC61850 standard: this new communication paradigm, which surpasses the limitations of serial port-based protocols in favor of abstract, independent data models, represents a significant turning point.

DaiTinEnergy, with its extensive experience in electrical engineering and specific communication and TCP/IP skills, is at the forefront of IEC61850 standard implementation. We offer a range of IEC61850 certified products, including protection relays and automation, monitoring, and control services with products such as RTUs, BCUs, and Gateways, accompanied by a complete suite of tools for configuration, integration, and testing. Our goal is to support customers in adopting flexible and intuitive solutions, facilitating the transition to IEC61850-based substation automation systems, minimizing interoperability risks and network inefficiencies.

Protection System Engineering Study

Proper selection, setting, and configuration of protection relays are essential for the safety, reliability, and efficiency of the electrical network, in addition to maximizing return on investment. DaiTinEnergy team, composed of expert application engineers, offers a comprehensive service for the selection, setting, configuration, and commissioning of protection relays. From short-circuit studies to protection coordination calculations, we provide solutions for optimizing the protection system, thus ensuring operator safety and service continuity.>

Our services include:

  • Protection scheme design
  • Selection of the most suitable DaiTinEnergy protection system for network needs and protection scheme
  • Design and provision of documentation and application diagrams
  • Setting of protection relays
  • From short-circuit studies to coordination calculations, DaiTinEnergy provides the best configuration of IEDs to isolate the faulty network section, prevent equipment damage, operator risks, and ensure service continuity.
  • Testing and commissioning of protection relays in the field.
